What is Connected Minds?

Connected Minds is a podcast created to explore how ideas become real-world outcomes. Across domains like InsurTech, FinTech, Payments, and Connected Vehicles, one truth stands out: there is never just one way to build or lead. Every culture, every team, every person brings a different way of thinking.

Each episode dives into how people solve complex problems, work with AI, lead teams, and navigate constant change ‚Äî sharing lessons that are honest, practical, and grounded in experience.

Meet Your Host

Ajay Chandran Jayachandrasekaran is a technologist and product leader who has worked across India, the United Kingdom, and Europe. His journey across multiple domains has shaped a simple belief: innovation thrives when diverse minds connect.
